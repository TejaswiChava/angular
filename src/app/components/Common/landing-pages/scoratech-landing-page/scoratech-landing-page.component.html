ABOUT SCORAXCHANGE

ScoraXchange is an eco-system of institutes, employers and students, that is efficient, evolving, and empowering to all its entities

It brings millions of institutes, companies and students from across the country, on a single, cloud-based platform and allows for a multi-faceted communication from each end

Despite the extensive data, the technological wonder allows each of its users to access their perfect fit within seconds, through intelligent filtering based on the requirements, skills and qualifications mentioned

Apart from this, the platform organizes and automates the hiring process for the employers, enables data-driven placements for institutes and provides a world of opportunities for the students.


WHY SCORAXCHANGE

A Revolution
like none other

ScoraXchange is a one-of-a-kind, multi-sided marketplace that revolutionizes the entire campus placement and recruitment industry.
It hosts an extensive database of institutes, students & companies and opens communications on all ends for a specifically targeted interaction.
The immense data that it hosts is technologically empowered for each user to filter their search to match their needs within no time.
This helps companies do away with a sea of data that is irrelevant, hence saving time, and for the students to scout for companies ranging from an international brand name located anywhere in the country, to a thriving start-up just around the corner.

Experience
Meets Innovation

ScoraXchange is the brain-child of industry veterans who have seen, experienced and even brought in transformation in technology.

Promise of state-of-the-art technological solutions for increased efficiency & accuracy, and innovation that can herald advancement.

Technology that revolutionizes the professional sphere by bringing immense data on the platform, opening focused communications on several ends, and organizing and automating the systems to reduce manual effort.

Foundation
of Principles

We host millions of students, higher-education institutes and employers to provide equal opportunities for every student, and to support efficient, data-driven recruitment.
We’ve built ScoraXchange with the idea of bringing in not only more opportunities, but the right ones, for both students and companies.
Not only this, through service providers on ScoraXchange, we aim to truly empower students to showcase and present their best to the prospective employers.
This coupled with a multi-faceted open communication, ScoraXchange ensures a vast range of opportunities and maximises the chances of a perfect career fit.
