Meet a world of oppurtunities

ScoraXchange is designed to aid students in three major ways. One, it empowers them with skill sets needed for internships, right to final placements. Two, it allows them to showcase all of their talents, skills and academic achievements in a detailed, and organised manner. Three, it opens up a world of career opportunities for them to explore, by hosting millions of companies from across the country. Despite the extensive data base of students and companies, the platform’s simple yet powerful search and filter tools helps the companies find just right student. It is like finding a needle in haystack, only within seconds.

Carve an edge

From the very first year of college, ScoraXchange brings in industry professionals to train and men-tor the students. To add to that early beginning, the platform also hosts service providers, that en-rich the students in more way than one, to make them better equipped for summer trainings, intern-ships, apprenticeships and final placements. With an edge like that, along with their academic achievements, the students come into the professional world with an edge that gives a boost to the start of their careers.

The perfect presentation

ScoraXchange allows students to make their personal digital profiles, listing all their personal infor-mation, skill sets, talents, academic achievements, projects and awards, certifications, and tal-ents.These profiles are not only detailed but also organised and efficient, in ways that it allows a student to present much more than just what they have achieved in college, hence making each profile stand out, when the employers look for candidates. All of this information, that the students have gathered through their lives, is available to search & filter from, to employers across the na-tion. Hence, when a student’s dream company is scouting for talent, the platform matches that need, to the student’s relevant achievement and makes a fit, within seconds.

Explore from a sea of options

After years of compromising, now, the students have a say in the kind of job best suited to their in-terests. As soon as a student logs in with their personal profile, they can explore millions of compa-nies in a vast range, to explore working opportunities from. Not only this, despite the extensive data, the intelligent filtering matches the students’ data with a company’s need and sends notification to the students every time an opening is announced that is relevant to them. And all of this, within a matter of seconds. So whether a student is a brand junkie, or a start-up enthusiast, no opportunity can hereby go amiss.
